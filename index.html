About Me

Developer & Creator

Hi, My name is Bilal. I am a recent graduate from Generation UK, a provider of Pre-Apprenticeship boot camps.

I enrolled in Software Development and have learnt a wealth of new things, thanks to an experienced instructor.

These 'things' include HTML, CSS, JS, TypeScript, Node.JS as well as certain work philosophies such as AGILE and frameworks, namely SCRUM.

As a result i have developed a working website portfolio, and attached are a handful of projects i have been working on over the intense 6 week course.

I am planning to make my way into this field, to further develop my skills as well as my self in an effort to change my life from one where i was experiencing verbal abuse on a regular basis to one of constant growth and creativity.

Prior to this I have self-taught the new spec MEI Mathematics and OCR Physics A-Levels, and am planning to take Further Mathematics in October of 2022.
